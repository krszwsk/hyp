Utilities

Classes that do things you would usually write yourself.

    <div class="full"></div>
    <!-- Pulls div to the left -->
    <div class="pull-left"></div>
    <!-- Pulls div to the right -->
    <div class="pull-right"></div>
    <!-- Centers everything inside div -->
    <div class="center-inside"></div>
